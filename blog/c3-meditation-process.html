Meditation - Deep Down, How Do You Really Feel...

Captivating thoughts from someone who tries not to think.
10 January 2017

Process Over Product
As builders like to say "measure twice, cut once". Working through a clear process of development before coding even begins allows the developer to think through their thoughts, work through any problem solving processes, design ideas, and confirm with the client. All part of the planning phase of creating. In the long run, this reduced the time it took for me to code up (only looking back now can I see that), gave me a foundation of what I was doing and how it would look (give or take). The process enabled me to look at what I was doing before I cut any wood.

Meditation
Before reading chapter 2 of the 'Search Inside Yourself' I thought of meditation as a process of claiming and clearing the mind in preparation to handle what comes ahead.

Learnings
Meditation trains two important faculties:

  • Attention
    • William James "taking possession by the mind, in clear and vivid form."
  • Meta-attention
    • ...is attention of attention, the ability to pay attention to attention itself. Or, put simply, knowing when you attention is wandering.

These two faculties enable you to be relaxed and alert at the same time. Hmmmm, I think I need to do some practice.

What Science Says About The Monks...
The Dali Lama lent 10 of his best meditating monks to a university to be studied. The university wished to study happiness. These monks were found to be the happiest people on earth. Personally, I find greater calmness after a meditation, but I'm sure I have happiness in there too.

Meditation Checklist
Meditation can be done by anyone, anywhere.

  1. Back straight "like an arrow"
  2. Legs crossed in "lotus position"
  3. Shoulders relaxed, held up and back, "like a vulture"
  4. Chin tucked in slightly, "like an iron hook"
  5. Eyes closed or gazing into space
  6. Tongue held against the upper palate
  7. Lips slightly apart, teeth not clenched

And if that sounds a little hard to start with, I simply sit in a comfy chair with a blanket and a cuppa tea, sure it might be cold by the time you get back to drinking it, but I'm ok with that.
