Reflection one - 01/12/16 My take on the DBC/EDA experience is that I need to pay attention. Phase 0 is my opportunity to really lay the ground work for the intensity of Phase 1. I am a little bit excited about it. My overall impressions are that this will be challenging, hard, exciting, ruthless, fun, challenging, exhausting, fun, evolutionary, tiring, and still fun. I see myself enjoying the process that this learning culture will bring up. I look forward to the new challenges it will raise both about me and for me. My expectations so far about EDA have not changed. I think everything is as it appears to me. This kind of learning environment definitely makes me nervous. For so, so many reasons. And for that alone I am excited.
