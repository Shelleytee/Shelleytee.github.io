Control Flow and Loops
The control flow is the order in which the computer executes statements in a script.

MDN - Mozilla Developer Network

Huh, you say? Well think of it like this. You wake up every morning, tumble outta of bed, stumble to the kitchen, pour yourself a cup of ambition, yawn and stretch and try to come to life. Jump into the shower and the blood starts pumpin, out on the street the traffic starts jumpin. Now suppose each of those actions were a script, the computer ensures that everything is done in the right order. This is called the control flow.
